ABOUT

BOYSKIDO

Adeyinka Oladunni Dare, known professionally as Boyskido, is a fast-rising Northern Nigerian Afrobeat artist and professional photographer born and raised in Sokoto State, though originally from Oyo State.

Known for his work in the Northern Nigerian music scene, he gained attention with hits like "Aure" (feat. Emtee Shmurda) and released the EP The Story of an Ordinary Sokoto Boy.
